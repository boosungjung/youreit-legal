# Privacy Policy for You're IT - Party Games

Effective date: March 2, 2026

You're IT - Party Games is designed for local party gameplay and does not require account sign-up.

## Data We Process

- **Purchase and subscription data**: processed by Apple App Store and RevenueCat to manage Pro access.
- **Device integrity and abuse-prevention data**: DeviceCheck tokens may be processed by our backend to help prevent repeated free-trial abuse.
- **Basic technical diagnostics**: limited technical logs may be generated by platform infrastructure for reliability and troubleshooting.

## Third-Party Services

- Apple (In-App Purchases, DeviceCheck)
- RevenueCat (subscription management)
- Cloudflare (backend hosting for DeviceCheck endpoints)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with service providers required to operate app functionality, purchases, and subscription status.
